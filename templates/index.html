About the Course

Welcome to IITMS College

IITMS College is designed to empower students with essential skills and knowledge to handle emergency situations effectively. Our comprehensive curriculum combines theoretical education with practical training to ensure students are ready to face real-world challenges.

The course covers fire safety, rescue techniques, disaster management, and emergency response strategies, preparing students for a rewarding career in firefighting and rescue operations.
