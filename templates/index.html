About the Course

Welcome to IITMS College

IITMS was formed under IITMS educational trust at Kollam, with one aim of delivering world class training for shipping, logistics, air cargo and supply chain management. The academy is backed up by an expert team of logistics professionals who have been working in logistics, shipping, airlines, freight forwarding, management and communication, warehousing, container freight station handling, retail and hypermarket segments and international trade export import companies for well over fifteen years and now active as trainers shaping professionals during the past decade.

In short, International Academy of Logistics is a cutting edge finishing school for logistics students and aspiring logistics professionals.
